What is a website?



A website is a collection of publicly accessible, interconnected web pages that share a single domain name. Websites can be created and maintained by an individual, group, business, or organization to serve multiple purposes. Together, all publicly accessible websites make up the World Wide Web. Although it is sometimes called a "web page", this definition is wrong because a website is made up of many web pages. A website is also known as a "web presence" or simply a "site".

Landing Page



Landing page can be translated as "destination page" - the first page on which an external user "lands", attracted to the site as part of certain marketing actions. But in order to better understand what a landing page is, it is important to describe how this type of subpages looks and their purpose. The main purpose of a landing page is to convince users to take a specific action on your web page.

Online shop



An online store or electronic store (e-shop), is an electronic commerce website intended for the sale of products and services. In most cases, the online store is a platform where products are added. These products can be accessed and purchased using your credit card. On complex online stores, users can select the payment method: credit card, bank transfer, cash on delivery and the transport method: post, courier, plane, etc.

Presentation Site



It is a possibility to communicate effectively with the target audience. It contains the key information about the company and its products – accurate arguments describing the advantages of a product or service, contact details and price list.

Site Catalog



To present products to a wide audience, but without spending a lot on developing an online store, a turnkey catalog site is the right solution.

Web design
